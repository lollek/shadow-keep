How To Play

Move: WASD
Strike: Left Click  ·  Guard/Parry: Right Click  ·  Dodge: Space
Throw shuriken: E  ·  Active item: Q  ·  Sneak: Shift  ·  Map: M

Visit shops in town between descents. Rare goods unlock at floor 5, epic goods at floor 10.
On boss floors, the exit is sealed until the boss dies.
Block during red enemy windups to parry, or circle behind enemies for backstabs.
